Platform · The Inspirelabs stack

One platform. Five properties. 200M+ shoppers a month.

Why we run our own platform

Most growth companies stitch together a SaaS stack. We do not. We run our own platform because the stack is the product, and outsourcing the stack means outsourcing the operating standard.

Twelve years of running consumer demand sites, six platforms in market, billions of intent signals every quarter: the only honest way to keep that operating standard high is to own the data layer, the distribution layer and the intelligence layer that compounds on top. Every offer page on GrabOn, every cashback decision on GrabCash, every creator commerce flow on GrabShare runs on Inspirelabs platform code we wrote ourselves.

Brands buy the network. The network runs on the platform. The platform is what we have actually been building for a decade.

Three layers, one platform

01Data layer

Where every signal lands.

Every click, scroll, search, redirect, install and purchase across the network lands in one warehouse. We process billions of events a quarter, attribute them at the offer level, and feed them back into ranking, ad allocation, cashback decisioning and creator payouts.

  • Real-time event pipeline
  • First-party identity graph
  • Server-side attribution
  • Offer-level revenue tracking
  • Cohort and LTV models
  • Privacy-preserving telemetry
02Distribution layer

Where intent meets the brand.

The distribution layer is five properties working in concert. Coupon-led discovery on GrabOn. Cashback-led conversion on GrabCash. Creator-led commerce on GrabShare. Buyer-led research on Alternatives.co. Search-revenue on RankDrive. One audience, five entry points, one operating standard.

  • 200M+ monthly users
  • 4,500+ brand partners
  • Creator network & payouts
  • Coupon & cashback engines
  • Editorial CMS at scale
  • SEO + GEO + SGE coverage
03Intelligence layer

Where the platform learns.

A fleet of 12 specialised agents reads the data, decides where to act and writes the next experiment. Every agent has a golden dataset, a regression suite and a human-in-the-loop reviewer. Nothing ships to production without an eval.

  • 12-agent fleet
  • Six-LLM content ensemble
  • Continuous regression evals
  • Production sampling
  • Human-in-loop reviewers
  • Brand voice guardrails

The agent fleet

The agent fleet is not a feature. It is the operating layer the rest of the company runs on. Six categories, 12 agents in production, more in eval.

AGENT/01 · CONTENT

Editorial drafting

Long-form drafting for offer pages, comparisons and category guides. Brand voice locked in eval.

AGENT/02 · SEARCH

Search intelligence

Reads SERPs, GEO citations and AI Overview surfaces. Writes the next-action queue for SEO.

AGENT/03 · OFFERS

Offer verification

Re-validates 200,000+ live offers daily. Flags expired, broken, restricted or fraudulent claims.

AGENT/04 · CREATIVE

Creative variants

Generates, A/B tests and ranks creatives across the affiliate and demand network.

AGENT/05 · ATTRIBUTION

Attribution review

Cross-checks server-side attribution events against rule-based and probabilistic models.

AGENT/06 · LIFECYCLE

Lifecycle orchestration

Selects the next email, push or in-app message based on per-user intent and product context.
