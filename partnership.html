How an engagement starts

No surprises, no theatrical pitch decks. Four steps from first email to a partnership that ships.

/STEP 01

First conversation

A 30-minute working call. You describe the growth problem. We say yes, no, or send you to the right partner.

/STEP 02

NDA & scoping

Mutual NDA, data-room access, and a written scoping document with the operating cadence, owners and deliverables.

/STEP 03

Pilot

A 60- to 90-day pilot with a published success metric. If the pilot does not clear the bar, we say so first.

/STEP 04

Launch

The pilot becomes a program. A named operator from our side joins your weekly review. The standing meeting becomes the contract.
