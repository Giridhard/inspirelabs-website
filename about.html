Twelve years in one room.

Inspirelabs started in a 600 sq ft office above a bookstore in Begumpet, Hyderabad. Eight people. One product. A line of credit on a personal card. The product was GrabOn. The brief was simple: build the most trusted coupons site in India, and earn every visit.

Twelve years on, GrabOn is one of five platforms we run. The other four are GrabCash, GrabShare, RankDrive and Alternatives.co. Together they route a billion conversions a year and have unlocked over ₹2,500 crore in savings for shoppers. None of it was paid for with outside money. Bootstrapped since the first hire. Profitable since year three.

The wall in the founder's room still carries the line we wrote in 2013. It is always Day 1. Day 2 is the day the work gets safer. The day a meeting replaces a decision. We are not interested in Day 2.

Three positions, since 2013.

The arguments we have not lost, and have not had to.

01

Operators outwork agencies.

An agency rents its attention. An operator stays with the problem. The people who write our roadmap also answer the support tickets. That is not a flex. It is the only way the work keeps getting better.

02

Bootstrapped is a longer game.

No investor clock. No quarterly theatre. We pay for the next quarter with the last one. The decisions that look slow on a one-year horizon look like the obvious ones on a ten-year one.

03

The bar only goes one way.

One standard, applied to every product, every brand reply, every push to production. The bar moves up every quarter. It does not move sideways. It does not move down. If a decision lowers it, we walk away from the decision.

Since 2013

A note from our Founder, Ashok Reddy

I wanted to build a company that earned its reputation slowly. Twelve years on, that is still the only rule.

Dear reader,

I started Inspirelabs in 2013 with no investors and no plan beyond the next twelve months. The first product was GrabOn. I gave myself one rule. If the work is useful, the rest follows. That rule is still the only rule.

Twelve years on, GrabOn is one of five platforms we run. The others are GrabCash, GrabShare, RankDrive and Alternatives.co. Together they route a billion conversions a year and have unlocked over ₹2,500 crore in savings for shoppers. We have not raised a rupee. Bootstrapped since the first hire. Profitable since year three.

In many ways we feel like we have broken some ice. We have shown that ordinary people with conviction and hard work can come together, defy the odds, and in under two decades build something durable. The journey has created real wealth, not just for me, but for the operators who built it with me and their families. That is the side-effect, not the goal. The goal has always been to do something worthwhile with the time we have.

The only company I am interested in is the one that exists in 2035. Not the one that wins the quarter that ends on Friday. If you are working with us, hiring us, partnering with us, or reading us for the first time, that is the frame. The work compounds when you give it the time it asks for. I have given it twelve. I plan to give it twelve more.

To everyone who has built this place with me — the operators, the brands, the shoppers, the families who put up with the late nights — thank you. The receipts are still stacking.

Ashok Reddy Founder, Inspirelabs Solutions Ltd.
