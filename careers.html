When we meet a candidate, we ask ourselves five questions.

The yes or no on each is the interview. The rest is paperwork.

  1. / 01 · SHARPER

    Are they smarter than us in the room?

    We hire to be raised, not to feel taller.

  2. / 02 · AMBITION

    Are they more ambitious than us about the work?

    Personal ambition is normal. Ambition for the work is rare. We need the second.

  3. / 03 · OWNERSHIP

    Do they take full ownership of their lives?

    The best operators don’t outsource agency. Nothing is anyone else’s fault.

  4. / 04 · HONESTY

    Are they comfortable saying, “I don’t know”?

    Bluffing is the most expensive habit in any room. We hire people who can stop.

  5. / 05 · THE ROOM TEST

    Will they make every person in the room sharper?

    Talent is a portfolio. The right hire raises the average. The wrong one drops it.

A decade in, the math changes.
