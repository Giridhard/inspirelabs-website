How the standard holds.

Four checks every operating decision passes before it reaches a customer.

01

A reviewed baseline.

A hand-curated standard for what "good" looks like, set by the senior operators who built it. Reviewed quarterly. The bar that everything else is measured against.

02

Continuous regression.

Every change runs against that baseline before it ships. If it regresses, it doesn't ship. There is no manual override.

03

Live sampling.

1 to 3% of every live decision is sampled, scored, and surfaced on a dashboard the founder reads weekly. Drift surfaces inside an hour.

04

Human-in-loop on every category.

Trained reviewers audit a slice of every category every week. Their decisions feed back into the baseline. The bar keeps rising.
