01 Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Inspirelabs Solutions Ltd. (the "company", "we", "us") and to the websites and applications operated by us, including GrabOn, GrabCash, GrabShare, RankDrive and Alternatives.co. Each property may also publish a property-specific notice; where there is conflict, the property-specific notice prevails for that service.

02 What we collect

We collect information you provide directly (such as account details, message content, brand-partner submissions), information generated as you use the services (such as device, log and approximate location data), and information from third parties where you authorised it (such as social or commerce accounts you connect).

  • Identity data: name, email address, role, employer.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, actions taken, search queries.
  • Device data: IP address, browser, operating system.
  • Commercial data: brand integrations, transactions, payouts.

03 How we use it

We use information to operate, secure and improve the services, to fulfil partner commitments, to communicate with you, and to comply with legal obligations.

In plain English

We use what we collect to make the products work, to keep them safe, and to send you what you asked for. We do not sell your personal data.

04 Legal basis

Where applicable law requires, we rely on consent, contract, legitimate interest, or compliance with a legal obligation. Where we rely on legitimate interest, we balance it against your rights and provide a way to object.

05 Sharing

We share information with service providers under contract, with the brand or platform you transact with where it is necessary to deliver the service, and with authorities where compelled by law.

In plain English

We share data with our hosting and analytics vendors, with the brand whose offer you redeem, and only with regulators when we are legally required to.

06 Cookies

Our properties use first-party and third-party cookies for authentication, preference storage, security, analytics and (where applicable) advertising attribution. You can manage cookies through your browser settings or via the property-level consent banner.

07 Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Aggregated data may be retained indefinitely.

08 Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, restrict processing of, or object to processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent.

In plain English

You can ask us what we hold about you, ask us to fix or delete it, or tell us to stop using it. Email privacy@inspirelabs.com and we will respond within 30 days.

09 Security

We maintain administrative, technical and physical safeguards aligned with SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001:2022. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat security as an operating standard, not a checklist.

10 International transfers

We are headquartered in India. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we use legally recognised mechanisms such as standard contractual clauses, supplemented by technical and organisational measures.

11 Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

12 Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified through the services or via email. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Questions? Email privacy@inspirelabs.com.
